The Installation Process

1

Decontamination

2

4

Application

Hand-applied liquid quartz flashing and leveling, locking in the finish permanently.

Crucial Curing Instructions

Ceramic coatings crystallize over time. While the vehicle is safe to drive shortly after application, strict care must be taken during the initial curing phase:

  • First 24 Hours: Keep vehicle dry. Avoid rain or sprinklers.
  • First 7 Days: Avoid bird droppings or tree sap. Wipe off immediately if exposed.
  • First 14 Days: Do not wash the vehicle with soap or chemicals.

The Maison Story

Based in the heart of Harare, Maison Detailing was born from a passion for automotive perfection. We recognized a gap in the market: traditional car washes were degrading clear coats, while dedicated studios required clients to leave their vehicles for days. We solved this by bringing studio-level detailing directly to your location.

Our approach is purely scientific. We utilize strictly pH-neutral, professional-grade chemicals, plush microfiber media, and spot-free filtered water to guarantee zero wash-induced marring. We don't just clean cars; we recondition them.
